EXPLAIN

A ratio is a comparison of two different thing or number. We generally express the two number as a ratio using a colon(:).
Example 1 from the situation given earlier:
If we want to compare:

  1. The pupil on board for 5-Perseverance to the total number of the pupil on board we write 30:45 (Read: 30 is to 45).
  2. The total number of pupils not on board the total number of pupils on board then it is 15:45(Read: 30 it to 45).
  3. The total number of pupils on board to the total number of pupils from both section we write it as 40:60 (Read: 40 is to 60).

Notice that the quantities in symbol are the same order as they are mention on the statement and that a colon is used to separate the two quantities. The colon is read “is to”

Example 2Anya was given twenty marbles of different colors:8 are pink , 7 are yellow and 5 are purple find the ratio of yellow marbles to purple marbles pink marbles to yellow marble and purple numbers to the number of marbles.

Statement Ratio
Yellow marble to purple marble 7:5
Pink marble to yellow marble 8:7
Purple to the total number of marble 5:20

Remember that yellow marble to purple marble (7:5) is different from the ratio of purple to yellow marble (5:7) ; Therefore it is important to consider the proper order given in the statement when writing ratios.
To verify this dividing 7 by 5 and 5 by 7. Did you get the same number? Certainly not.
A ratio is also defined as the quotient of the quantity divided by the second quantity. Therefor a ratio is also a fraction. The first quantity is placed in the numerator while the second quantity is place to denominator.
So to express the ratio of yellow marble to purple marble we can write It as 7/5.
