Do you know what 0.25 means? Study how 0.25 is written on the place value chart. Study the chart below.

In 0.25, the digit is a place holder for the ones place. The digit 2 in the tenths place. Its value is 0.2. The digit 5 is in the hundredths place, its value is 0.05. Hence, 0.25 means 2 tenths and 5 hundredths,
The decimal point separates the whole number from the decimal numbers.

Here are other examples;
In 0.56,5 is in the tenths place. Its value is 0.5.

Six is in the hundredths place. Its value is 0.06.

In 0.09,0 in in the tenths place. Nine in the hundredths place.

Its value is 0.09.
