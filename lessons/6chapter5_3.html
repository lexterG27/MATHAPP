Lesson 3 - Subtraction of Integers

ENGAGE

The temperature in Baguio City was 12°C in the morning. It dropped to 8°C in the evening. What is the difference between these temperature?

EXPLORE

To get difference between the two temperatures, we need to subtract 8°C from 12°C. We can formulate the number sentence
(+12) – (+8) =n.

Subtraction integers is adding the opposite of the subtrahend to the minuend. In this case, the opposite of +8 is. We add -8 to +12, giving us +4. Therefore, Baguio City’s temperatures between morning and evening differ 4°C.

EXPLAIN

Referring to the previous example, we can see that by using the operation on subtraction we arrived at the answer which is 4. One of the ways to subtract integers is by using example.

Example
(+5) – (+2)=__

Take away 2 black tiles from 5 black tiles. Three black tiles remain.
Answer:+3

KEY IDEAS
1. Two integers are opposites if their distance from 0 on the number line are the same.
2. A number and its opposite are called additive inverses.
3. In subtracting integers, add the additive inverse of the subtrahend to the minuend.
