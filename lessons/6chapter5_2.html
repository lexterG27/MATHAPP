Lesson 2 - Addition of Integers

ENGAGE

Mrs. Reyes bought fruits which cost ₱ 700.00 from a wholesaler and sold them in her fruit stand. On Monday , her sales was ₱ 800.00; and on Tuesday, ₱ 500.00.But on Wednesday, she lost ₱ 400.00 because some of the fruits were already rotten. Considering the sales from Monday to Wednesday, did Mrs. Reyes gain or lose?

EXPLORE

To know whether Mrs. Reyes gained or lost, we have to combine her total sales which will be then be compared with her capital. By solving we have:

(+800)+(+500)+(-400)=900

Therefore, the total sales from Monday to Wednesday is ₱ 900.00.Comparing this to the capital of ₱ 700.00, we can say that Mrs. Reyes gained ₱ 200.00. Since her total sales of ₱ 900.00 is more than her capital which is ₱ 700.00.

EXPLAIN

Addition is the same as “combining”. How do we combine integers? Study the number line below.

Examples
(+4)+(+3) =+7
(-4)+(-3)=-7
(-7)+4=-3

In using the number line for adding integers:

• Always start at zero.
• Add a positive integer by moving to the right on the number line.
• Add a positive integer by moving to the left on the number line.
