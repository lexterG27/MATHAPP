Lesson 2 - Place value and Value of a Digit in Numbers up to 100 000

EXPLORE

In 2013, Barangay San Sebastian harvested 64 789 kilograms of mangoes.What does each digit in 64 789 mean? Study how 64 789 is written in the place value chart.


The digit 9 is in the ones place. Its value is 9.
The digit 8 is in the tens place. Its value is 80.
The digit 7 is in the hundreds place. Its value is 700.
The digit 4 is in the ones place. Its value is 4000.
The digit 6 is in the ten thousands place. It value is 60 000.

The value of a digit is determined by multiplying that digit by its place value.
