Lesson 2 - Renaming Decimals to Fractions.

EXPLORE

As a decimal, 0.7 has one digit after the decimal point.

So, you rename 0.7 as 7/10 in fraction.

Look at how 0.14 is written as a fraction. The decimal number 0.14 is written as 14/100 in fraction form as shown in the hundred squares.
