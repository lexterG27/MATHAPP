Mang Timoteo cut a bamboo pole in to 10 equal parts and maid them in to 10 coin banks. If he solved some and had 6/10 of the coin bank left, what decimal part of the coin bank was left?

How do you rename a fraction to a decimal number?

The illustration shows that 6 coin banks were sold out of 10 or 6/10.How do you rename 6/10 to a decimal number?

Notice that the denominator 10 has 0ne zero. The number of zeros in the denominator is equal to the number of decimal places after the decimal point in a decimal number.
1) ½ = 5/10 = 0.5
2) 1/5 = 2/10 = 0.2
3)3/5 = 6/10 = 0.6
4) 4/5 = 8/10 = 0.8

How will you write 35/100 as a decimal? 8/100?

Look at how these fraction are written as decimals.
1) 8/100= 32/100 = 0.32
2) 9/20 = 45/100 = 0.45
3)12/50 = 24/100 = 0.24
4) 3/50 = 6/100 =0.06
