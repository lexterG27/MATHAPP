Lesson 5 - Solving Real-Life Problems Involving Whole Numbers

ENGAGE

There are 4 Bus Liner A and 1 Bus Liner B that were hired at ₱ 11,445.00 each to take 280 students to the Palarong pambansa venue. What was the transportation cost per pupil?

EXPLORE

George Polya is a Hungarian mathematician who, in 1945, formulated steps in problem solving namely: understand the problem, make a plan, carry out the plan, and look back, Now let us solve the problem above using these steps.
Step 1 – Understand

  1. What is asked in the problem?
    • The transportation cost per pupil.
  2. What are given?
    • 4 Bus Liner A and 1 Bus Liner B
    • ₱ 11,445,00 paid for hiring 1 Bus
    • There are 280 students
  3. What are the hidden question?
    • How many buses were used?
    • How much was paid for all buses?

Step 2 - Make a Plan

  1. What operations will be used?
    • addition, multiplication, and division
  2. What is the mathematical sentence?
    • (4+1)x 11 445] ÷ 280=N(the transportation per pupil)

Step 3 - Carry out the Plan

Since the number sentence has more than one operation , we will apply PMDAS rule in order to properly solve for N
[(4 + 1) x 11 445] ÷ 280 = N
(5 x 11 445) ÷ 280 = N
57 225 ÷ 280 = N
204.375 = N

Therefore. each pupil’s transportion cost ₱204.375, which is approximately ₱204.40

Step 4 - Look Back

Is the answer reasonable? Estimate to check:
60 000 ÷ 300 is 200
204.375 is near 200
Therefore, the answer is reasonable.
