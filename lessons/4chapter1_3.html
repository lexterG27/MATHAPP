Lesson 3 - Reading and Writing Numbers up to 100 000 in Symbols and in Words

EXPLORE

The Bureau of Forest Development donated three seedlings to each pupil in Region 2. The total number of seedlings donated was 64 821.
How do you read and write the number 64 821 in words and in symbol?
The number 64 821 is read as “sixty-four thousand eight hundred twenty-one”. In symbol, it is 64 821.
